Welcome to the official portal of Dalocha Woreda. This platform is dedicated to making our district's development, social services, and future plans accessible to everyone.

ፕላን | Our Plan

የምግብ ዋስትናን ማረጋገጥ፣ የመሰረተ-ልማት ግንባታዎችን ማፋጠን እና የጥቃቅንና አነስተኛ ኢንተርፕራይዞችን ማበረታታት የወቅቱ ዋና እቅዶቻችን ናቸው።

Ensuring food security, accelerating infrastructure development, and encouraging micro & small enterprises are our current major strategic goals.
